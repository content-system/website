Job Brief

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Java Technical Lead to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for leading and overseeing the development of Java-based applications, ensuring the highest standards of quality and performance. As a Java Technical Lead, you will play a pivotal role in guiding our projects from conception to completion, working closely with cross-functional teams to deliver cutting-edge solutions that meet our clients’ needs.

Responsibilities

  • Lead the design, development, and deployment of Java-based applications, ensuring adherence to best practices and coding standards.
  • Provide technical guidance and mentorship to team members, fostering a collaborative and innovative development environment.
  • Collaborate with architects, product managers, and other stakeholders to define technical requirements and project scope.
  • Conduct code reviews and ensure the quality of code deliverables, identifying and addressing issues promptly.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends, technologies, and best practices, and incorporate them into the development process.
  • Design and implement scalable, secure, and maintainable system architectures for Java-based applications.
  • Ensure the integration of software components and third-party applications into the overall system architecture.
  • Optimize application performance, scalability, and reliability through effective architectural design and implementation.
  • Collaborate with DevOps teams to establish and maintain continuous integration and delivery pipelines.

Job Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field; a Master’s degree is a plus.
  • Proven experience as a Java Technical Lead, Senior Java Developer, or similar role within the technology sector.
  • Extensive experience in designing, developing, and deploying Java-based applications.
  • Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) and microservices architecture.
  • Demonstrated experience in leading and managing cross-functional teams.
  • Experience in Java/Python and related technologies (Spring, JPA, Java EE, etc.).
  • Have experience in front-end technologies (e.g., HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Angular, React) is a plus.
  • Strong knowledge of software development tools and practices, including version control (e.g., Git), CI/CD pipelines, and automated testing.
  • Experience with relational and NoSQL databases
  • Experience with GRPC and REST.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively engage with stakeholders at all levels.
  • Demonstrated leadership capabilities, with the ability to inspire and motivate cross-functional teams.
  • Passion for understanding and solving customer needs and delivering exceptional user experiences.

Nice to have

  • Healthcare domain knowledge.
  • Strong understanding of Agile methodologies, including Scrum and Kanban.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with a data-driven approach to decision making.
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment.
